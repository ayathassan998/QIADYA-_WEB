Promising leaders

for a thriving economy

With the Kingdom's Vision 2030, and based on research studies that highlight the impact of women in leadership positions on the prosperity of organizations and the economy, the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia’s leadership set ambitious goals that provide women with opportunities to be part of the country’s growth and development.

QIADYA Program’s mission is to increase the proportion of Saudi women in leadership positions.

Driven by our nation's ambitious vision in supporting the advancement of women leaders in all fields, QIADYA Program seeks to enhance the leadership capacities of women through skills training and development that will empower them to lead effectively and navigate through the unique challenges they face in the Saudi labor market.

To achieve the overarching goal, QIADYA Program includes the following objectives:

Understanding the latest concepts and frameworks in leadership.

Developing skills and capabilities such as team leadership, delegation, negotiation, and communication.

Developing skills and capabilities such as team leadership, delegation, negotiation, and communication.

Examining approaches to leading organizations and change.

Broadening knowledge on digital leadership and its impactful role on the future of KSA.

Building a supportive professional network.

Illuminating women’s experiences as leaders in KSA and exploring the challenges women face as leaders in the Saudi work environment..
